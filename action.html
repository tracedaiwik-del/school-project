🌱 Be Part of the Solution

How You Can Help

Small actions, taken together, create enormous change. Here's your local action plan to fight plastic pollution starting today.

5 Simple Steps

Start Your Eco Journey Today

These practical steps can dramatically reduce your plastic footprint

01
🍶

Use Reusable Bottles

Carry a reusable water bottle every day. One person using a reusable bottle can prevent 156 single-use plastic bottles from entering the waste stream every year.

02
👜

Say No to Plastic Bags

Keep cloth or jute bags handy for shopping. Politely decline plastic bags at stores. Each cloth bag replaces hundreds of plastic bags over its lifetime.

03
♻️

Recycle Properly

Learn your local recycling rules. Clean and sort plastic before recycling. Not all plastics are recyclable — look for the recycling symbol and number.

04
🧹

Join Clean-Up Drives

Participate in local beach, river, or park clean-ups. Even a 20-minute clean-up in your neighbourhood can make a visible difference to your community.

05
📢

Spread Awareness in School

Share what you've learned with classmates. Organize eco-awareness events, create posters, or start a Green Club at your school. Your voice matters!

06
🍱

Bring Your Own Container

Use stainless steel or glass containers for takeaway food. Encourage family members and local restaurants to adopt eco-friendly packaging alternatives.

Weekly Challenges

Your Weekly Eco Challenges

Challenge yourself and your friends — every week, pick one and commit to it!

🚫

Plastic-Free Week

Go an entire week without buying anything that comes in single-use plastic. Plan meals, carry your own bags and bottles.

🍶

Bring Your Own Bottle

For 7 days straight, refuse to buy any bottled water. Carry your reusable bottle everywhere — school, outings, everywhere!

♻️

Recycle 10 Items

Collect and properly recycle at least 10 plastic items this week. Wash them, sort them, and drop them at your local recycling point.

🗑️

Neighbourhood Clean-Up

Spend 30 minutes picking up plastic litter from your street, park, or local area. Invite a friend to join you!

📲

Share 3 Eco Facts

Share three plastic pollution facts with your friends or family every day for a week. Awareness is the first step to change.

🌿

Plant Something

Plant a tree, shrub, or herb this week. Reuse old plastic containers as pots to give them a second life as planters!
